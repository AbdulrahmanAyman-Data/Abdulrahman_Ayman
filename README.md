# Hi there, I'm Abdelrhman! 👋

###  Data Engineer | GIS Analyst | Spatial Data Enthusiast

I began my journey with data in the world of **GIS**, where I first discovered the power of spatial databases. Starting with spatial analysis and database management, I learned how to look at the world through maps and coordinates.

This curiosity naturally led me into **Data Analytics**, where I saw firsthand how data-driven decisions can solve complex problems. However, I soon realized that great insights are impossible without high-quality, reliable data. This realization was my turning point into **Data Engineering**.

Today, I truly enjoy building the "behind-the-scenes" pipelines that collect, clean, and sync data. My goal is to ensure that data is always a reliable asset ready to drive impact.
